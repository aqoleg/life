Game of Life

One instance of a cellular automaton with certain rules:

  • 2-dimensional space of cells. In this instance opposite edges are contiguous. So it represents torus surface.
  • Cells have two states - dead and alive (green).
  • Each cell has 8 neighbours - Moore neighborhood.
  • On the next step any cell with 3 neighbours and any alive cell with 2 neighbours will be alive, every other cell will be dead. Rule B3/S23.
